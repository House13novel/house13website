About the Author

Christopher Castonguay

About Christopher

Christopher Castonguay is a debut novelist whose fascination with consciousness, reality, and the deeper questions of existence led him to craft House 13. Drawing from his own journey of exploring metaphysical concepts and spiritual inquiry, Christopher has created a work that speaks to anyone who has ever questioned the nature of their reality.

The inspiration for House 13 came from Christopher's personal exploration of themes around identity, reincarnation, and the interconnectedness of all existence. The novel serves as both an entertaining adventure and a philosophical exploration, particularly meaningful for readers navigating their own existential questions.

When not writing about infinite buildings and cosmic journeys, Christopher enjoys hiking, exploring philosophical texts, and engaging in conversations about consciousness and the nature of reality. He believes that storytelling can be a powerful tool for examining life's deepest questions and hopes that House 13 offers readers both entertainment and insight on their own journey of discovery.

House 13 is Christopher's first published novel, representing years of exploration into the questions that fascinate him most: Who are we? Why are we here? And what transforms when we begin to truly examine the nature of our existence?
