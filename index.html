About the Novel

In an infinite building where every door leads to a new challenge, a newcomer must learn the rules of a reality that defies expectation. In the House, nothing is quite what it seems and certainty is scarcely found.

A metaphysical adventure spanning multiple lifetimes, House 13 follows souls as they navigate a cosmic journey where each floor tests different aspects of consciousness. From rooms that demand physical courage to challenges requiring spiritual wisdom, every experience shapes the hero's understanding of who they are.

Perfect for readers of visionary fiction and spiritual adventure, this novel explores reincarnation, free will, and the nature of reality. As characters discover that kindness and cruelty, wisdom and ignorance, are perhaps different paths up the same infinite staircase, they—and readers—must confront the ultimate question:

What transforms when you begin to explore the mysteries of consciousness, identity, and existence itself?
