The Mission

House 13 was written specifically for those grappling with metaphysical solipsism and existential questions about the nature of reality. The novel serves as both entertainment and a potential roadmap for those feeling lost in questions about consciousness, identity, and existence.

If this work has provided comfort, insight, or companionship during your own journey of questioning, your support helps continue this exploration through future writing and research.
